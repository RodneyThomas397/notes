Force Notes


What is a Force?

A force is a push or pull that can make an object move


What is mass?

Mass is the measure of how many atoms an object has, this is a fixed value, it does not change based on location.


What is weight?

Weight is the force of gravity pulling an object down


Are mass and weight the same?

No!!! Mass is value that does not change, an object no matter where it is will always have the same amount of stuff, weight is dependent on and changes with gravity

3. What happens to the weight of the cannonball on the moon?

Mass stays the same, the overall “size” of the cannonball does not change from the moon to earth

Weight will change, the cannonball will weigh less on the moon due to a smaller gravity



Newton’s 1st Law of Motion

An object in motion stays in motion until acted upon by an outside force. An object at rest, stays at rest until acted upon by an outside force.
